Our Services

Functional Behavioral Assessment

A Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) will assess your child on a variety of skills and behaviors. This process will identify the most important behaviors and skills to include in your child’s treatment plan. This plan is designed to meet your child’s individual needs targeting problem behaviors to reduce, appropriate skills to be taught, and strategies to promote success.

In home 1:1 ABA therapy

After a thorough assessment, a Board Certified Behavior Analyst will design and oversee a comprehensive treatment plan to address your child’s needs. You child and family will get 1:1 ABA therapy at home targeting the reduction of problem behaviors and teaching functional replacement skills.

In school, daycare, and community services

A comprehensive treatment plan helps your child find success in all environments, not just your home. We can help in schools, daycares, or any community activities where your child needs assistance. Often specific training is needed in different environments and we can target needs in each setting.

Parent Training

Training is available to help parents learn how to analyze their own child’s behavior and how to use proven strategies to increase overall compliance, teach more appropriate skills, and respond to inappropriate behaviors.

IEP Advocacy

If your child receives Exceptional Student Services and has an Individualized Education Plan (IEP), it can be overwhelming to navigage the education system alone. We can walk you through the process, provide insight, attend meetings, and help you ensure your child receives the education they deserve!

Professional Development

Staff training available to teach staff more about working with children with disabilities, behavioral challenges, or even general classroom management strategies. Trainings can be tailored to your specific needs.
